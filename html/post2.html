My mum is so cool: Part 2

Published: 14.07.2022
My mum is so cool! | Brown

Today it's going to be about day 2 of my mum's visit.

The weather was not great at all - it was raining a bit and it was cold. What's more, there is not much to do in Finnmark if you don't like nature and you prefer staying at a café. Well, be aware, it's not going to happen here. Take it or leave it.

Nature then! We drove to Trollholmsund and Stuorra Guoskat and enjoyed the view. Both rocks formations and view over the fjord were beautiful, despite rain and cold.

I'm not sure why I chose to go full in when it comes to browns - it's pretty green here during the summers, so I really didn't need to be so dramatic! On the other hand, this palette (Brushed Courdroy, Walnut Stain and Vintage Photo) really good reflects the mood of that day.

When it comes to idea for this layout, I wanted to communicate the arrival of my mum. Therefore you can spot a plane as well as a destination for her travel on the top of the world's map.

And what about documenting itself? Well, it might be difficult to spot, but there are some notes behind the photos.
