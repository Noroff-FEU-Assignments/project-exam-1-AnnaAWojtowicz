My mum is so cool: Part 3

Published: 05.03.2022
My mum is so cool! | Brown

Day 3. Day 3 of the cloudy sky and rain every now and then. But we don't give up, or do we?

Of course we don't! We put the warm clothes on, kidnapped our friend's dog and set off towards a natural conservation area near our place which is called Stabbursnes. We wanted to show my mum where we pick up mushrooms every autumn but also a few places where you can watch birds. We were also lucky enough to spot some reindeers - it wasn't the first time we did it, but it always fascinating and fun to see them.

This layout took me a long time to make, but I had a really great time during the process and I like how it turned out.

Firstly, I started with the background - I used Tim Holtz 3D embossing folder that gave craft paper wooden planks texture. I used also another 3D embossing folder to cut out letters that imitated type bars of an old typewriter.

The most time consuming part of this layout was, with any doubt, a shadow box. Cutting out the trees from dyed paper and placing a photo between them took its time, but the result was worth the effort!

In this project I used:

  • Ground Espresso - Distress Ink
  • Vintage Photo - Distress Ink
  • Lumber - Sizzix (#662718)
  • Typewriter - Sizzix (#664760)
  • Countdown - Sizzix (#665367)
  • Christmas Shadow Box - Sizzix (#663611)
  • Reindeer - MAKIStamps
  • Tiny Things 2 - Stamper Anonymous (CMS305)
