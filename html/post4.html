My mum is so cool: Part 4

Published: 05.04.2022
My mum is so cool! | Grey | Red | Black

Enough with browns! Let's do something different today!

We were a bit bored with all that rain following us wherever we went. We had to try something different - like travel further, like escape the rain. Did it work?

Yes and no.

Firstly, we went to Honningsvåg - a little charming town, placed roughly 30km from the northernmost point in Europe. Though the town was indeed charming, there was something more exciting waiting for us there. The North Cape. Everybody who is in the area should do it. Not because of the plateau with the globe-monument and the building with a café and souvenir shop. Those are ok, but overrated. What your guide probably won't mention is getting there. The way taking you to the North Cape is breathtaking. Trust me, I've taken it many times. And in this case the quote "The journey is the destination" is so much true!

As much as we liked the journey, we didn't manage to escape the rain. It was following us the whole way up to the north (and back) and when we finally got there, along with the rain there was strong wind as well. So it was cold and windy. Perfect! Not.

All this inspired me to use grey as my main colour this time. The colour - Hickory Smoke - perfectly matched my irritation caused by the shitty weather. However, the layout needed some contrast - therefore I also used Aged Mohagony and added some black and white to put all together. The effect? Well, you tell me!

The last thing I want to mention is a faux-postcard I created to do some journaling. The idea with the postcard worked out fine and a hand-made post stamp reminds me of reindeers we came across while driving to the north.

And this is an example for which I like documenting my adventures - even the tiniest element represents memories that stay with me.
