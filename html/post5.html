My mum is so cool: Part 5

Published: 05.11.2022
My mum is so cool! | Hike | Orange | Brown | Blue

Karma finally gets my mum.

When I was a teenager, my used to make me go out for a stroll. At that time I was so cool that I truly believed that watching tv was so much better than being close to nature. You can imagine how many times I opted out, wanted to return in the middle of the stroll or just wanted the world to end. Nothing of those things happened. Ok, now fast-forward twenty years or so.

That day we were a little bit tired after the trip to the North Cape so my partner decided to stay at home. Me and my mum, on the other hand, cycled to the foot of the plateau that outstretches beside our village. There we started our hike to get to the top of the plateau and watch a place where a waterfall starts.

We were ascending the top pretty slowly, taking few pauses to enjoy the view or plucking and eating cloudberries. Even though at some point my mum got tired. She wanted to opt out. Or return. Or the world to end. Nothing of those things happened and at the mum, we reached the top.

The colour of rocks looked much alike rust and therefore I mostly used Rusty Hinge in this project. It went so good with orange cloudberries and brown rocks, and even blue sky. Not bad! This combo of colours will be used more in the future.

In this project I used:
